📟 NasDisplay Firmware Updater

ESP32-C6 | Waveshare 1.47" Touch LCD

This tool will flash the latest firmware to your NasDisplay device via Web Serial.
No additional software is required – just a compatible browser (Chrome, Edge, Opera).

⚠️ Your browser does not support Web Serial. Please use Chrome, Edge, or Opera.
🔒 Web Serial requires HTTPS or localhost. Please use GitHub Pages or a secure server.
🟢 Ready. Connect your NasDisplay (ESP32-C6) via USB and click the button above.
📌 Instructions
1. Connect your NasDisplay to the computer using a USB‑C cable.
2. If your operating system requires drivers for the USB‑to‑serial chip, install them (CP210x or CH340).
3. Click the Connect & Flash button.
4. In the dialog, select the correct serial port (e.g., COM3 or /dev/ttyUSB0) and click Connect.
5. Wait for the flashing process to finish. Do not disconnect until completion.
6. After success, the device will restart and the display should show the NAS interface.
⚠️ Important Notes
• The firmware is designed for the ESP32-C6-DevKitM-1 board with the Waveshare 1.47" display.
• Make sure the UART connection to your NAS uses 3.3V levels (no RS‑232 without level shifter).
• After flashing, the device will run the default demonstration until it receives data via UART.
• For full functionality, connect a UART cable from your NAS to pins 16 (RX) and 17 (TX) with GND.
